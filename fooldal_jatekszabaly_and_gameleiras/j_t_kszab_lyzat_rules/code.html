Regulatory Framework v4.2

RULES OF ENGAGEMENT

The Obsidian Astral is a persistent universe built on respect, competition, and technological excellence. By entering the void, you agree to uphold the Hegemony Protocol and act with honor within the digital frontier.

Fair Play & Combat

  • 01. Exploiting game mechanics to gain an unintended tactical advantage over other Pilots is strictly prohibited.
  • 02. Automation of combat via third-party scripts or neural-link overlays will result in immediate exile.
  • 03. Win-trading or "Ghosting" during Clan Wars is considered treason against the Astral Hegemony.

System Comms

  • 01. Hate speech, harassment, or targeting individuals based on their biological or digital origin is not tolerated.
  • 02. Spamming global broadcast frequencies or advertising external black markets is prohibited.
  • 03. Impersonating Astral Hegemony Enforcers or System Administrators will result in permanent comms black-out.

Economy Integrity

  • 01. Real-Money Trading (RMT) of Astral Credits or Rare Artifacts outside the official Hegemony Exchange is banned.
  • 02. Market manipulation through large-scale botting or coordinated artificial scarcity is a severe offense.
  • 03. Account selling or transferring Pilot identity is restricted for the security of the clan ecosystem.

Security Protocols

  • 01. Attempting to breach the Astral server core or reverse-engineer client code is a Level 5 Violation.
  • 02. Sharing credential hacking tools or malicious link injections within the community is grounds for total IP ban.
  • 03. Failure to report a discovered critical system vulnerability ("Zero-Day") for personal gain is punishable.

Banning Policy & Escalation

The Obsidian Astral employs a three-tier enforcement system designed to rehabilitate where possible and excise where necessary.

Tier 1: Warning

Initial breach results in a 24-hour suspension and formal reprimand on the Pilot's record.

Tier 2: Quarantine

Repeat offenses lead to a 7-day server lockout and 50% credit forfeiture.

Tier 3: Exile

Permanent termination of all assets and access. No appeal process for Level 3 violations.
